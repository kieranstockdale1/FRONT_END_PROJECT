Welcome!

This website is dedicated to all things ice hockey. It contains information on the rules of the sport, the teams in the National Hockey League (NHL) in America, the teams in the National Ice Hockey League (NIHL) in England and Wales and a way to contact us for more information on ice hockey in your area.

The rules page takes you through all of the rules of the game, to give novices to the sport a better understanding of how the game is played and what actions are legal.

The page on the NHL explains the format on the competition and provides you with links to each organisation's home page.

The NIHL page is very similar to the NHL page, providing you with information on the competition and links to the teams websites.

Finally, the contact us page allows you to contact us with any queries you may have about the sport, such as your local ice hockey team and how you can get more involved in the sport.
