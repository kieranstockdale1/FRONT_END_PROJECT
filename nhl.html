NHL

The NHL (National Hockey League) is the best hockey league in the world, containing teams from North America, but hosting players from all around the world. Because of this international appeal, and the way that the league is set up it is very unusual that a team becomes dominant in the league for many years, unlike in other sports. The league is set up in a playoff format, which means at the end of the regular season 16 of the 31 teams play for a chance to win the Stanley Cup. The most recent champions of the NHL are the Pittsburgh Penguins, but the most prestigious team is the Montreal Canadiens, winning the title 25 times.
